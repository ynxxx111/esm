About Us

Enjoy Sweet Moments

At Gooddays Sweets, we believe life’s truest joys are found in Every Sweet Moment. Since 1999, we have been dedicated to designing and manufacturing innovative candy toys that transform ordinary moments into delightful memories for children and families across 48 countries and regions.

As a global leader in candy toy manufacturing, we specialize in crafting imaginative and high quality products in key categories: Hard Candy, Liquid Candy and Gummy Candy. Our expertise extends from developing licensed products for the world’s top confectionery brands to building our own beloved brand portfolio supported by an expanding international network.

Every product we create reflects our commitment to safety, creativity, and joy. We uphold the highest quality standards, holding certifications including ISO 9001, ISO 22000, BRC, SMETA, HALAL, Disney, and Walmart, and our products consistently meet or exceed stringent regulations such as FDA, ASTM, and European EN71 Part 1–3.

From concept to consumption, we’re here to make moments sweeter—because at Gooddays Sweets, we don’t just make candy. We create memories, spark smiles, and celebrate the simple, sweet moments that bring us together.
